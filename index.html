One button. Could be: Register for camp | Claim free trial | Refer a friend

Market Trends & Intelligence
What's trending in US youth basketball, AAU, and social media — updated perspective as of 2025
📈 What's Trending in Youth Basketball (2025)
Training & Program Trends
  • ▶ Skills-over-wins culture: Parents increasingly value development programs over just W-L records
  • ▶ Mental performance training: Huge growth — mindset coaches becoming standard
  • ▶ Film study at youth level: Growing trend — BTG already does this — market it harder
  • ▶ Smaller, boutique programs outperforming factory clubs in parent satisfaction
  • ▶ Data/analytics for youth: Video analysis tools, shot tracking gaining adoption
Social Media Trends in Youth Sports
  • ▶ Player-generated content: Athletes documenting their own journey performs best
  • ▶ Authenticity over production: Raw phone footage outperforms studio-produced clips on Reels/TikTok
  • ▶ Parent content explosion: "Youth sports parent" content is one of the fastest-growing niches on TikTok/IG
  • ▶ Short-form dominant: Under 30-sec wins. YouTube Shorts growing fast.
  • ▶ NIL awareness among youth: Players 16+ increasingly think about personal brand
AAU / Club Basketball Trends
  • ▶ AAU oversaturation: Parents skeptical of pure AAU — opportunity for development-first clubs like BTG
  • ▶ College recruiting shifting younger: Players being identified at U12–U14 levels
  • ▶ Academic + athletic balance: Parents prioritize programs that respect school commitments
  • ▶ Travel team burnout awareness: Growing backlash to over-traveling — local programs benefit
BTG Opportunity Areas
  • ▶ Position BTG as the anti-AAU factory — development-first, family-culture messaging
  • ▶ Own the "mindset + hoops" niche in LA — nobody else is doing it as explicitly
  • ▶ Partner with Westside HS coaches for referral pipeline
  • ▶ Create parent education content — huge underserved gap in LA youth sports market
